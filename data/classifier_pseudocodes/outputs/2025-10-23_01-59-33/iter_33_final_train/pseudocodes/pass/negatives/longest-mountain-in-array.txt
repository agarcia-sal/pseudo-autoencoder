```
n ← length(arr)
longest ← 0
i ← 1
while i < n-1:
    if arr[i-1] < arr[i] > arr[i+1]:
        left ← i-1
        right ← i+1
        while left > 0 and arr[left-1] < arr[left]:
            left ← left - 1
        while right < n-1 and arr[right] > arr[right+1]:
            right ← right + 1
        longest ← max(longest, right - left + 1)
        i ← right
    else:
        i ← i + 1
return longest
```
