CLASS Solution  
    FUNCTION nextPermutation(nums)  
        SET i TO LENGTH OF nums MINUS 2  
        WHILE i IS GREATER THAN OR EQUAL TO 0 AND nums AT i IS GREATER THAN OR EQUAL TO nums AT i PLUS 1  
            DECREMENT i BY 1  
        IF i IS GREATER THAN OR EQUAL TO 0  
            SET j TO LENGTH OF nums MINUS 1  
            WHILE nums AT j IS LESS THAN OR EQUAL TO nums AT i  
                DECREMENT j BY 1  
            SWAP nums AT i WITH nums AT j  
        REVERSE THE SUBSEQUENCE OF nums FROM i PLUS 1 TO END  
    END FUNCTION  
END CLASS
