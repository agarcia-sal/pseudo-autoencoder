CLASS Solution  
    FUNCTION connectTwoGroups(cost)  
        SET size1 TO length of cost  
        SET size2 TO length of first element in cost  
        INITIALIZE dp AS a two-dimensional list with dimensions size1 PLUS 1 by TWO raised TO size2, FILLED WITH infinity  
        SET dp at position 0,0 TO 0  
        
        FOR i FROM 0 TO size1 MINUS 1  
            FOR mask FROM 0 TO TWO raised TO size2 MINUS 1  
                FOR j FROM 0 TO size2 MINUS 1  
                    SET dp at i PLUS 1 and mask OR (TWO raised TO j) TO minimum of dp at i PLUS 1 and mask OR (TWO raised TO j) AND dp at i and mask PLUS cost at i and j  
                FOR j FROM 0 TO size2 MINUS 1  
                    IF mask AND (TWO raised TO j) IS NOT EQUAL TO 0  
                        SET dp at i PLUS 1 and mask TO minimum of dp at i PLUS 1 and mask AND dp at i PLUS 1 and mask XOR (TWO raised TO j) PLUS cost at i and j  
        
        RETURN dp at size1 and (TWO raised TO size2 MINUS 1)  
    END FUNCTION  
END CLASS
